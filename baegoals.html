Bae Goals | Ui & Graphic Design

Our Bagels Before Anyone Else

Created: First Semester of BCIT Digital Design and Development Program | September 2018
Class: Design Fundamentals
This project was to create a brand identity and create an entire style guide and final mockups for a website. I decided to create "Bae Goals" which target young and hip students in the Granville Island Area. What made my designs stand out was that all the images were hand drawn by myself, scanned in, and edited with photoshop and illustrator.

Tools Used

  • Illustrator
  • Balsamic
  • Photoshop

Goals:

  • Create a brand
  • Create a styleguide
  • Design web layouts

Styleguide

Out of all the designs and websites in my class, Bae Goals stood out due to this styleguide I created. All images were hand drawn and then scanned into the computer for editing. This gave the bagel shop a unique feel and "artsy" feeling I was aiming for. The website is very clean, but with a twist. My style definitely came out during this project, which is adding little drawings/details that add the the white space without being too crowded.
