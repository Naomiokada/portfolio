About Me

As a creative individual, I am constantly finding ways to express myself and let the world see my art. After receiving my graphic design certificate at BCIT, I decided to start my journey in web development and digital design. I am currently a student in the digital design and development program at BCIT; therefore, I live and breathe design everyday. From a young age, I knew my creativity and passion inside would not be put out. And I am finally pursing that.

Through my schooling and small freelance work in graphic design, I've honed my skills in branding (creating style guides), typography, illustration, layout design, and concept development. I have a strong passion for design, and seeing them come to life in the real world is my dream.
