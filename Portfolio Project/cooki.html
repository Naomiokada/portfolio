Cooki. Web App

Interactive Cookie Recipe Generator

During my second semester of my Digital Design and Development Program, our final project was to design and devlopment an app. My team (a group of three) came up with Cooki and was passionate about making this app stand out.

Goal: To create an easier and more efficient way to search up recipes, as well as making it a fun user experience

My Role

  • Lead Graphic & UI Designer
  • Co Project Manager
  • Animator and illustrator

Design & Layout

Cooki's Design is animation and illustration heavy so we used a lot of white space to give it a clean look. Our target audience was towards 25-35 year old's who enjoy a clean and minimilistic interface. Our animations and illustrations let us get away with a minimilistic design without looking too plain or empty.
