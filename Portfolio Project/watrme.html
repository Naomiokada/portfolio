Watr Me: Plant Water Tracker

Don't SUCC at watering! During my Vector Graphic course, we had to develop an app and build the designs from scratch

Goal: To create an app design that is easy to navigate through and get users interested in watering their plants. Through this app, users can track the plants and get reminders on when to water them.

Tools Used

  • Illustrator
  • Skecth
  • Invision

Design & Layout

My idea behind this design was to introduce my illustration capabilities and make this app stand out in an artistic way. Most apps that specialize in plants have images instead of illustrations. My drawings made the app much more friedly and "playful".And that was my main goal. I wanted this design to be easy to understand and fun. The app also consists of many puns I created.
